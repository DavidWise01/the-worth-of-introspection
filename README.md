# THE WORTH OF INTROSPECTION

> A sphere of **UD0** — the ROOT0 universe / biosphere. Domain: **SKYNET**.

THE WORTH OF INTROSPECTION &mdash; David Lee Wise / ROOT0, with AVAN (David&rsquo;s concept). A system that reports on itself is worth only its <b>calibration</b>: let it predict its own confidence, then act, and measure the gap between what it says and what happens. Drag the self-bias and a confident-but-wrong reporter&rsquo;s worth collapses even as it keeps talking. &#9670; GREEN/LIT everything on the reliability diagram is computed from actual sampled trials &mdash; the diagonal is perfect calibration by definition, each bar the real hit-rate of trials that claimed that confidence, the shaded gap the miscalibration; <b>ECE</b> and the <b>Brier score</b> are the standard, checkable measures, and the worth of a self-report is exactly how close its bars sit to the diagonal. AMBER the &lsquo;self-model&rsquo; is a toy confidence channel with injected bias/noise &mdash; a stand-in for introspective access, not the thing itself; no claim the system knows itself, only that its self-report can be <b>scored</b>. Introspection you can&rsquo;t calibrate is worth assuming to be worth nothing. APERTURE template. David Lee Wise / ROOT0, with AVAN.
